At Bahas, we take the privacy of our users very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of information we collect, how we use that information, and how we protect our users' data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information except gmail addresses from our users. We do not require users to provide their real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifiable information. We do, however, collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, device information, and usage data to help us improve our application.

How We Use Your Information

The non-personal information we collect is used to help us improve our application, such as identifying areas of the application that need improvement or analysing user behaviour to make the application more user-friendly. We may also use this information to generate statistical reports about our user base.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect our users' data. We also regularly review and update our security practices to ensure that our users' data is protected from unauthorised access or disclosure.

Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information with third-party companies or organisations. We may, however, share non-personal information such as usage data with third-party analytics providers to help us improve our application.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve the user experience of our application. Cookies are small files that are stored on a user's device to help the application remember user preferences and settings. Users can choose to disable cookies in their browser settings.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes to the Privacy Policy through the application.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or the data we collect, please contact us at bahasutils@gmail.com . We are committed to protecting our users' privacy and will do our best to address any concerns in a timely and effective manner.
